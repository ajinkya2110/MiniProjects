DECCAN GYMKHANA

Where Sports and Health is a Way of Life.

Deccan Gymkhana emanates an ambiance that fosters fresh ideas within a backdrop of inviting coziness.
For generations, families have cherished the exceptional sports amenities and services offered by Deccan Gymkhana, creating an unparalleled membership experience.
At the heart of Deccan Gymkhana's allure lies its captivating 'At Home' atmosphere, complemented by the distinction of its esteemed members.

GUESTS ROOM

There are total 11 Guest Rooms (10 Deluxe
Rooms and 1 Suite). Guest rooms are
furnished with Twin Beds, Air-Conditioner,
Refrigerator, and Television Set and has an
attached Bath & Toilet.

Guest Rooms are available to any Guest of
Members as well as Members of Outstation
Affiliated Clubs.

CONFERENCE HALL

Deccan Gymkhana provides an Air-
Conditioned Hall for Meetings and
Workshops.

Conference Hall is equipped with Audio-
Visual aids including a Video projector. It can
accommodate up to 60 persons.

Maximum Capacity of 50-60 PAX.
