DAILY POOJA ROUTINE

Shree Ambabai (Mahalaxmi) Temple, Daily Puja Schedule

Rituals Timings Description
Mandir Opening Time 04:00 am -
Padyapuja & Mukhmarjan 05:00 am -
Kaakad Aarti 05:30 am to 06:00 am Naivedya: - Butter and sugarAfter the opening of the temple in the morning, the Goddess KakadAarti
begins at 5.30 am with mouthwash and foot worship of the Goddess, offering
butter and sugar.
Morning Mahapuja 08:00 am At 8.30 am the bell is rung and Goddess Panchamrut Abhishek,
Shodshopchar Pooja is performed and KhiriNaivaidya is offered.
Holy Food (Naivedya) 09:30 am -
Afternoon Mahapuja 11:30 am Panchamrut Abhishek Shodshopchar Pooja is performed at 11.30 am and
Mahanaivaidya is offered at Puranpoli on Friday at 8.15 pm Mangalarati is
started.
ALankar Puja 01:30 pm Alankar Puja is performed by offering Mahavastra to the Goddess and
wearing traditional gold ornaments on the body of the Goddess.
Dhuparti 08:00 pm The bell rings at 8 pm and starts at 8.15 am and after offering to
Shri,LaduKaranja'sNaivaidya, Shankhtirtha is performed.
Shejarti 10:00 pm Neighbors used to offer milk and sugar at 10.15 pm. The temple closes at
10.30 pm.
